Velyne's fried pork

Look at that yummy yum porky pork!

Here's the ingredients you'll need!

  • Pork (of course)!
  • Onion
  • Tomatos
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt (and lots of it)!

Here's how we do it!

  1. First, cut some of the yummy fat off the top and fry it until the fat comes out.
  2. Next, cook the onion in the fat on medium heat
  3. Now add the ginger and garlic and let it sizzle for a min
  4. Add the tomato next and let it turn to mush-mush
  5. Finally, add the qubed pork and let that baby cook
  6. Super finally, eat!
